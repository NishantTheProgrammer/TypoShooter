How To Play

  • Type "start" to start the game
  • For moving crosshair type the name of direction and then space and then value which you want to move in pixels. Ex: left 40
  • When your crosshair at the the target type "shoot"

Scoring Scheem

  • The points are compound of two things
  • Remaining Time + Distance between crosshair and archery
  • 1 Remaining second = 2 points
  • crosshair 0px far from archery = 100 points
  • To score high type fast and be accurate to your target
